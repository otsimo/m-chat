The M-CHAT is validated for screening toddlers between 16 and 30 months of age, to assess risk for autism spectrum disorders (ASD). The primary goal of the M-CHAT was to maximize sensitivity, meaning to detect as many cases of ASD as possible. We will return the test result learned from analysis of the your data.
