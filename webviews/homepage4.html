How This Study Works

This study will require you to:

  1. Complete Eligibility Tasks
  2. Complete M-Chat Screening
  3. Result & Save

This study is designed for persons over 18 years old who can be Parent, Grandparent, Caregiver, Guardian, Health Personel or Teacher.

1. Eligibility
You need to have the app on your phone in order to participate in this study. Everyone who enrolls will first complete a consent process explaining the risks and benefits of the study. As part of this process, you will also confirm your agreement to participate in the study. Afterward, we will ask you some questions about the study to complete the process. Eligibility will include asking some question about the child (age, etc.).

2. MChat Screening
We will ask you to answer questions about the child, mental and physical condition, and current status. You may choose to leave test any time, you do not wish to answer.

3. Result & Save
You can see your result at the end of the test. Also, you can save or share it.

All tasks should take you no more than 10 minutes. You have the right to refuse to answer particular questions or participate in particular aspects of the study.
